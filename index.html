Contact Us

We are looking for microbiologists, food scientists, computer engineers, software developers, programmers, and bioinformaticians. If you are interested to join, collaborate, or inquire, please do not hesitate to email peggy.woh@polyu.edu.hk.
Undergraduate students: To discuss potential research projects or ideas, feel free to reach out to Peggy and arrange a brief meeting.
Graduate students: Prospective students interested in pursuing a Master or PhD program in Food Safety or Food Technology (AI) at Hong Kong Polytechnic University can submit a CV along with a brief statement of their research interest before applying. They can arrange a discussion with Peggy in advance.
Other positions: The lab is always actively looking for interns, researchers, and developers. If you are interested in doing these positions, please do not hesitate to email Peggy to discuss.
Office: Department of Food Science and Nutrition, Faculty of Science, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Hung Hom, Kowloon, Hong Kong.
